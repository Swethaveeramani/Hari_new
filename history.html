HISTORY

Know Our Roots

1957

Born as a Nobleman

Founder, Mr. Hari was born in 1957 in a small village in Cuddalore, Tamil Nadu, India.

Growing up in a traditional South Indian family, he inherited his passion, knowledge, and love for cooking from his father who was a cook at his own hotel.

Travel for the Destiny

In 1971, Mr.Hari made the courageous decision to support his family, prompting him to journey to Erode in search of a job. His passion for cooking, which was ingained in his being, led him to Mr. Gopal Iyer, and it was here that his fervour for the culinary arts flourished. Day by day, his dedication to cooking continued to blossom.

Creation of the territory

In 1979, Mr Hari was determined to realize his ambitions of preparing the food he desired and working for a business of which he could be proud, so he established Hari Catering Service.

First Ever Buffet Service at Erode

Year 1982 was the era when Erode and its vicinities were presented only with dining options by caterers for any occasion. Mr. Hari astounded the attendees of a wedding event by providing the 5-Star Hotel experience with a buffet set up.

This remarkable feat elevated the Catering Industry in Erode and revolutionized the way clients perceived caterers.

Fest on Institutional Catering

In 1987, we commenced providing canteen facilities at BVB Schools across all three of its campuses. Preparing meals for the children heightened our sense of responsibility and necessitated us to take an even more discerning look at our standards of food preparation, thereby raising the profile of Hari Catering Service among parents.

Launch of Production Unit

In 2009, a Production facility was established at Thottipalayam, taking up an area of 1 Acre amidst lush surroundings.

This stimulating and thriving workspace is equipped with an in-house kitchen and storerooms for vessels, utensils, cooktops, and buffet stalls, granting us the capacity to prepare food for a maximum of 10,000 people.
